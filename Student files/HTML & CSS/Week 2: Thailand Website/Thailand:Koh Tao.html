Ko Tao



Ko Tao is an island in Thailand. Restaurants, nightspots and dive shops cluster in Mae Haad Beach and near Sai Ri Beach.
Villas dot palm-fringed beaches such as Chalok Baan Kao Bay, on the south coast.
In the northwest, Koh Nang Yuan is a group of islets with hilltop views. Ko Tao is known for its tropical coral reefs,
with species including whale sharks and rays. Hawksbill and green turtles gather here to breed.
