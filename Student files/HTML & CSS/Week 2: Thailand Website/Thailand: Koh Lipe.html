Koh Lipe



Ko Lipe is a Thai island in the Andaman Sea, near Malaysia's border. It's part of Tarutao National Marine Park,
which is populated with small islands and known for its coral-rich waters. The island has sandy beaches,
including Pattaya and Hat Chao Le. Dive sites in the area include the 8 Mile Rock pinnacle and the Yong Hua Shipwreck.
Ko Lipe is home to many aboriginal Malay people, known as the Urak Lawoi.
